Rules & Regulations

Each round will last 30 minutes.

Participants must complete typing the provided paragraph within this time frame.

The event is open to all who wish to participate.

Participants will receive a paragraph that they must type.

Participants must type the paragraph correctly as fast as possible.

Time limit: 30 minutes.
